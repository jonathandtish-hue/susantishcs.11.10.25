What Makes It Different

Principles of Christian Science Healing

These ideas are drawn from the Bible and explained thoroughly in Science and Health with Key to the Scriptures by Mary Baker Eddy. They show why healing is natural and repeatable.

Start with spiritual fact

We look beyond material symptoms and affirm what God knows about you—whole, beloved, and strong. This awakens receptivity to healing.

Let prayer reveal solutions

Prayer is more than petition. It’s listening for the Christ—spiritual truth that dissolves fear and reveals the answer already present.

Expect permanent results

Because divine law is absolute, the healing it brings is stable. Many experience immediate change; others notice steady, unstoppable progress.

A Living Tradition

Rooted in Christ Jesus’ example

Christian Science follows the healing ministry Jesus taught—seeing everyone as spiritual, whole, and inseparable from God’s goodness.

Christ Jesus’ promise

“He that believes in me will do the works that I have been doing…” (John 14:12). Jesus proved that spiritual identity brings freedom here and now.

Mary Baker Eddy’s discovery

In the 1800s, Eddy articulated the divine laws behind Jesus’ healing and showed how anyone can apply them. Her textbook, Science and Health, remains a guide worldwide.

Modern-day practice

Christian Science practitioners devote their lives to this healing ministry—praying with those who reach out and witnessing transformations daily.

Your healing journey

As we pray together, we drop limiting views of ourselves and accept our inseparable link to God, like a sunbeam to the sun. This shift brings tangible change.

Thousands of verified healings—from chronic illness to financial hardship—are published every year in The Christian Science Journal and Sentinel. These accounts illustrate the reliability of prayer when based on divine law.
