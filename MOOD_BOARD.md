# MOOD BOARD — WARM, WELCOMING, SPIRITUALLY CALM WEBSITE

## Overall Aesthetic:
A peaceful, uplifting digital space that feels like walking into a sunlit room.
Soft light, natural colors, gentle curves, and calm typography create an environment
of healing, reassurance, and trust. Nothing is sharp, loud, or corporate.
Everything feels intentional, human, and serene.

## Color Palette:
- **Warm neutrals:** cream (#FAF8F3), linen (#EEE9DF), warm beige (#E9E3D3)
- **Soft greens:** sage (#C7D8C6), eucalyptus (#AFCBB9), moss (#9EB8A5)
- **Calming blues:** sky blue (#D8EAF4), dusty teal (#86A8A6), mist blue (#C7DDF0)
- **Accent warmth:** muted gold (#D9C79E), sand (#E2D7C0)

## Textures & Backgrounds:
- Very subtle paper texture or grain
- Light watercolor swashes
- Soft gradients (cream → sage, cream → sky blue)
- Gentle shadows like morning light

## Typography:
- **Headings:** elegant serif (Playfair Display, Lora, or EB Garamond)
- **Body:** soft humanist sans-serif (Inter, Lato, Source Sans)
- Layout uses large, open headings with gentle spacing
- Emotional tone: clarity, grace, and warmth

## Layout Style:
- Varied section shapes (split layout, full-width, staggered blocks)
- Alternating color backgrounds for gentle rhythm
- Wide but not overwhelming content sections
- Avoids rigid, repetitive card grids
- Includes moments of visual storytelling and breathing room

## Imagery:
- Nature-led: leaves, trees, sky, sunlight, flowing water, soft landscapes
- Soft focus or lightly blurred backgrounds
- Human touches without staged stock photos
- Light, airy, and uplifting

## Shapes & Components:
- Rounded corners (rounded-xl or rounded-3xl)
- Soft pill-shaped buttons
- Organic curves, wavy separators, flowing lines
- Minimal boxes; more layered, natural layouts

## Tone & Emotion:
- Gentle, compassionate, encouraging
- Quiet confidence
- Welcoming, peaceful, spiritually grounded
- A sense of hope, clarity, and healing

## Interaction:
- Soft fade-ins and slide-ups
- Cushion-like hover states
- Slow, smooth transitions
- Nothing snappy or abrupt

---

**The site should feel like calm water + warm sunlight + an open door.**
